How Studio works

  1. Set up your bag with average carry per club. No shot file required. Import a launch monitor CSV later if you want more accurate spin, smash, and consistency.
  2. Add courses from the shared library or upload your own greens JSON.
  3. Open your caddie for club calls, Compare, Insights, and Live GPS.

Yardage books

Printable yardage books still use the OpenYardage desktop pipeline (OSM + elevation + PDF). Studio gives you the AI Caddie app with your data. Export a course pack from Setup when you are ready to generate PDFs locally.
